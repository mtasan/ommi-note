# Privacy Policy for Asyra

**Last updated:** March 1, 2026

## Overview

Asyra is a voice-first note-taking app. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

Asyra does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.

- All notes are stored **locally on your device** using SQLite.
- Voice recordings are processed temporarily for transcription and are **not stored on any server**.

## Voice Processing

When you record a voice note, the audio is sent to Google Gemini API for transcription. The audio is processed in real-time and is **not retained** by the service after processing.

## Microphone Access

Asyra requests microphone access solely for the purpose of recording voice notes. Microphone data is never used for any other purpose.

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Gemini API**: Used for voice-to-text transcription and AI-powered note extraction. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Storage

All user data (notes, reminders, preferences) is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers.

## Children's Privacy

Asyra does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
